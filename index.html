Ingredients

  • 6 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon mustard
  • 3 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • 1 pinch of salt and pepper
  • Paprika for garnish

Directions

  1. Boil the eggs for 10 minutes.
  2. Peel them and cut them in half.
  3. Mix the yolks with mayonnaise and mustard.
  4. Fill the egg whites with the mixture.
  5. Sprinkle with paprika and chives.
